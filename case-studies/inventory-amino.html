Real Inventory Project

Inventory Amino

A mobile-first inventory system for lab stock management, designed to improve stock visibility, reduce picking errors, and make low-stock issues visible before they interrupt work.

Context

Lab inventory was tracked across spreadsheets and manual checks, making it hard to know what was available right now.

System Design

Built with AppSheet and Google Spreadsheet for fast adoption, mobile access, and low operational overhead.

Business Value

Improved stock visibility, reduced wasted time searching, and created a foundation for reorder and usage reporting.

What Changed

  • Centralized item records with validation rules and a mobile-friendly lookup flow.
  • Added real-time stock updates and low-stock alerts to reduce surprise shortages.
  • Made item discovery faster with image-based references and standardized records.
  • Created a practical base for reorder review and future inventory analytics.
